Why Cardio?

Cardio workouts improve heart health, increase endurance, and help in weight management.

  • ✅ Strengthens heart & lungs
  • ✅ Aids in weight loss
  • ✅ Reduces stress & anxiety
  • ✅ Boosts energy levels

Best Cardio Workouts

High-intensity workouts for quick fat loss.

Swimming

Low-impact but effective for weight loss.
